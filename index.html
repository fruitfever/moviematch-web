MovieMatch

MovieMatch is an app designed for movie enthusiasts who are looking for an easy way to find and discover new movies. The app offers a range of features, including suggestions for similar movies based on your favourites. This allows you to explore new films that you may not have otherwise discovered.
In addition, MovieMatch offers a search feature that allows you to quickly and easily find movies. Once you find a movie that interests you, you can check out its overview and plot to see if it's something you want to watch.
